Crypto Scalping Strategy (5-Minute Timeframe)
Timeframe
5-minute chart
Indicators Used
9 EMA (Exponential Moving Average)
15 EMA (Exponential Moving Average)
Buy Conditions

Generate a Buy Signal when all the following conditions are met:

The 9 EMA crosses above the 15 EMA (bullish crossover).
At the crossover point, the angle/slope of the EMA movement is 30° or greater, indicating sufficient momentum.
The market should not be in a sideways or low-volatility condition.
Sell Conditions

Generate a Sell Signal when all the following conditions are met:

The 15 EMA crosses above the 9 EMA (bearish crossover), or equivalently, the 9 EMA crosses below the 15 EMA.
At the crossover point, the angle/slope of the EMA movement is 30° or greater, confirming strong bearish momentum.
The market should not be in a sideways or ranging condition.
Sideways Market Filter

The bot must avoid generating signals during sideways (ranging) market conditions. Signals should only be triggered when the market shows a clear trend with sufficient momentum.

Strategy Objective

The strategy is designed for crypto scalping, aiming to capture short-term price movements using EMA crossovers combined with momentum (angle) confirmation while filtering out false signals in sideways markets.

Bot Logic Summary
Buy → 9 EMA crosses above 15 EMA + angle ≥ 30° + market not sideways
Sell → 9 EMA crosses below 15 EMA + angle ≥ 30° + market not sideways
No Trade → Sideways/ranging market detected
